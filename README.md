# PoundSchool – Affordable Education Platform

**Author:** Saleem Shaik  
**Role:** Founder & Technical Lead  
**Project Start:** August 2024  

## Overview
PoundSchool is a concept platform designed to make education, mental health
resources, and learning materials accessible through a low-cost “£1 knowledge
store” model.

The project focuses on affordability, fairness, and broad accessibility across
the UK.

## My Role
I conceived the platform and led the technical design, including the pricing
model, access control strategy, and system architecture.

## Innovation
The innovation lies in designing a pricing and access model that balances
sustainability with extreme affordability, enabling wide access to educational
and wellbeing content.

## Project Status
This project is in the design and pre-implementation phase.
